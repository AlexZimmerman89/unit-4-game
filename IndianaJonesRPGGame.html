Fight the Mola Ram

Instructions

Welcome back to 1984 fellow adventurers! The evil Mola Ram will use his black magic to give you a totally random number at the beginning of each round.
Each time you click on one of the good characters, a specific amount of points will be added to your total score.
You can beat Mola Ram by matching your total score to his number. You will lose the game if your total score goes above Mola Ram's number.
The value of each of the good characters is hidden from you until you click on one of them.
Each time the game starts, the values of each good character will change.
